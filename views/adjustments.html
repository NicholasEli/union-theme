With all the different boot sizes, shapes, and rider preferences - fine tuned sizing and fit adjustments are paramount for absolute performance and comfort. The following are a some of the key components that make up "Union Fit".

Vaporlite

"Full Bottom" Vaporlite EVA Bushings provide exceptional shock absorption while maintaining supreme board feel with natural board flex.

FC Vaporlite 3.0

Forged Carbon and Vaporlite 2.0 combine to give the best of both worlds - a super stiff platform, cushioned with the highest quality dampening available anywhere.

CP3 Highback

Developed with Gigi Rüf, the all new CP3 Highback is lighter than previous models, and comes equipped with the new "Mini FLAD" forward lean adjuster.

Minidisk

Minimal binding-to-board footprint crucially reduces interference with your board's true flex, providing a more natural response and feel. 4x2 and Channel ready.

Exoframe

Independent suspension and support create a seamless interface. This all new super cushioned and lightweight design comfortably holds you in, but won't hold you back.

Magnesium 2.0

Four times stronger than aluminum with only half the weight, Union buckles are built to last. The all new 2.0 design is 10% lighter than previous versions.

Ultragrip X

All new for 16/ 17, UltraGrip X is the evolution of our thermoplastic anti-slip toe straps. Lighter, grippier, and even more comfortable.
